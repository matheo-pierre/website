Our Story

A Bike Story came to me from a childhood idea, I remember when I was 12 my father made me a bike with his own hands. This bike was my very first and I still have to this day, but above all it was unique and had a real story. Of all my friends I was the only one to perceive this precise feeling when I climbed on my bike. Today, our goal is to create your personalized motorcycle, the one you will cherish, that will make you feel unique.
